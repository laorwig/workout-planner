Coach tips
Progressive overload: Add 5 lbs or 1 extra rep to at least one exercise per week. Check Progress before each session to know what to beat.
The faded rep: The mark nobody sees is the one that builds character. Show up when you don't have to.
Post-workout window: Eat protein within 45 min of finishing. This is when your muscles absorb nutrients most effectively.
Sleep: Early morning workouts demand consistent bedtimes. 7–9 hours drives fat loss and muscle repair more than any supplement.
